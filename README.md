# Saout

A football talent scouting platform designed to connect young Saudi football players with professional scouts and clubs through data-driven player profiles, performance tracking, and streamlined talent discovery.

## Overview

Saout is a digital platform created to bridge the gap between school leagues, football academies, and professional clubs in Saudi Arabia. Many talented young players remain undiscovered due to limited visibility and the lack of structured communication between emerging talents and professional scouts.

The platform provides coaches with tools to document player development, manage team performance, and create detailed player profiles. Scouts can explore player data, analyze performance statistics, review match information, and identify promising talents more efficiently.

By creating a centralized scouting ecosystem, Saout supports the development of Saudi football talent and contributes to building a stronger pathway toward professional-level competition.

## Problem Statement

Saudi Arabia has many talented young football players in school leagues and academies, but many opportunities are lost due to limited exposure and inefficient scouting processes.

Saout addresses this challenge by providing a platform where:

* Coaches can showcase player abilities and development
* Scouts can discover and evaluate emerging talents
* Clubs can build a more organized and data-driven scouting pipeline

## Key Features

### For Coaches

* Create and manage player profiles
* Upload player information and academic details
* Track player statistics and performance
* Manage team rosters and match lineups
* Add match results and records
* Upload player highlight videos

### For Scouts

* Discover players from academies and school leagues
* View detailed player profiles
* Analyze statistics and performance data
* Watch player highlight reels
* Review match lineups and results
* Compare player performance using visual charts
* Schedule match visits to evaluate players in person
